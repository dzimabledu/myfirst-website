CGPA and GPA Calculator

GPA (Grade Point Average) and CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) are academic measures used to evaluate a student's performance. They are often used by educational institutions to assess the overall academic achievements of students.

To calculate GPA, follow these steps:

  1. Input the number of subjects.
  2. For each subject, enter the subject name, credit hours, and grade point.
  3. Click the "Calculate GPA" button to see the result.

To calculate CGPA, the process is similar. Instead of calculating for individual subjects, you'll input all subjects taken across multiple semesters or academic periods. The total grade points and total credit hours are then used to calculate the CGPA.
